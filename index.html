About Us

How We Started

Founded in 2010, Nairobi Fresh Milk began with a single dairy farm in Kiambu County. Our founder, John Kamau, wanted to bring fresh, high-quality milk directly to Nairobi residents.

Today, we work with over 50 small-scale dairy farmers across Kenya's highlands to bring you the freshest milk possible.

Our Dairy Farms

Our milk comes from small-scale farms where cows are pasture-raised and treated with care. We ensure:

  • No artificial hormones
  • Regular veterinary care
  • Ethical treatment of animals
  • Sustainable farming practices

From Farm to Table

Our milk goes through a careful process to ensure quality and freshness:

  1. Morning milking at 5am
  2. Immediate cooling to 4°C
  3. Quality testing at our facility
  4. Pasteurization and packaging
  5. Delivery to vending machines within 12 hours
